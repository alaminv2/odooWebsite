    All about Smart Group

    Welcome to our Smart Family

    In 1998 Smart Group set foot in Bangladesh ICT market as Smart Technologies (BD) Ltd providing technology solutions. Going through enormous challenges and exceptional business strategy Smart Group achieved its reputed sustainable position nationally and internationally.

    Starting from a seed, Smart Family is now a large tree with several branches providing shade and support to millions. When so many companies face it difficult to sustain a long-term business, we, the Smarts proved to be the Dark Horse running front line with business innovations and out of box thinking.

    We uphold our highest quality and professional service to maintain customer satisfaction and be loyal to our clients and partners. As a fast-driven company, Smart Group upgrade, change and improvise with the market need and nourish its position in the industry.

    Apart from ICT product distributions, over the years with business expansion Smart Group consistently involves in other business arenas such as printing solutions, construction and engineering, retail chain shop, software development, stationery item distribution, agriculture, education etc.

    Today Smart Group is flourishing with more than 1800 employees, 2000 partners, 72 brands, 28 branches and many more to go.
